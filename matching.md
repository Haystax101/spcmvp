# How Supercharged Matching Works

## Overview

Supercharged uses a two-part matching system to find people you'll genuinely connect with. It combines **AI-powered understanding of who you are** with **specific profile details** to surface the right matches at the right time.

---

## The Two Pillars: Meaning + Details

### 1. Semantic Understanding (What AI Learns About You)

When you fill out your profile, Supercharged's AI reads between the lines to understand the deeper version of you—not just the words you type.

**What the AI picks up:**
- **Your goals and ambitions** — If you write "building a climate tech startup," the AI understands you're entrepreneurial, environmentally conscious, and technical.
- **How you think and work** — "I prefer deep work and long-term thinking" tells the AI you value focus and sustainable effort, not quick wins.
- **What you're looking for in connections** — "Research collaborators in biology" reveals you're intellectually curious and collaborative, not just looking for biology nerds.
- **Your values** — Mentions of fairness, impact, learning, or community get picked up as core to who you are.

The AI creates an invisible "ideal profile" for the people you'd work well with—based on complementary traits, shared values, and how you actually think.

### 2. Specific Profile Sections (The Hard Facts)

Alongside AI understanding, matching also looks at concrete information:

- **College & subject** — Direct commonalities (same college, overlapping subjects)
- **Goals** — Do your goals overlap or complement?
- **Desired connections** — What type of person are you looking for?
- **Skills & interests** — Music taste, hobbies, social circles
- **Career stage** — Are you both in similar positions or complementary ones?

These sections act as **filters and boosters**:
- Same college = +24 points (familiarity)
- Matching career field = +12 points (shared world)
- Overlapping hobbies = +X points per shared interest

## How Matching Actually Happens

### Step 1: AI Creates Your "Ideal Match Profile"

When you complete your profile, Supercharged uses AI to synthesize your words, goals, and interests into a mental model of:
- The person you'd naturally click with
- The complementary traits that would make collaboration work
- The values and ways of thinking that align with yours

This isn't a literal checklist—it's more like "people similar to these types I describe" or "people who share this kind of thinking."

### Step 2: The Four Dimensions of Compatibility

Every potential match is scored across four dimensions. You see these as the four coloured dots next to each person's name:

#### **Identity** (Purple)
How similar is who you fundamentally are?
- Same college, similar background, shared core interests
- Do you "get" each other at a basic level?

#### **Intent** (Blue)
Do your goals and ambitions align or complement?
- Are you both entrepreneurs, or is one building and one investing?
- Do your definitions of success match?

#### **Resonance** (Teal)
Do you click on a personal level?
- Similar hobbies, music taste, ways of thinking
- Can you imagine grabbing coffee and actually enjoying it?

#### **Network** (Orange)
Do you know overlapping people or circles?
- Same social circles, societies, or communities at Oxford
- This creates natural conversation bridges

Each dimension scores 0–100. Your overall match score is a blend of all four.

### Step 3: Surfacing Matches

Supercharged doesn't show you random people. The "Surfaced for You" feed uses:

1. **AI-generated ideal profiles** → finds semantically similar people
2. **The four dimensions** → scores how compatible you actually are
3. **Recency & activity** → prioritizes people who are active
4. **Serendipity** → occasionally shows you people slightly outside your usual type (to avoid filter bubbles)

---

## Why This Matters

**Semantic matching alone** would be too vague. You'd match with people who use similar words but want completely different things.

**Detail matching alone** would be too narrow. You'd only match with people who share your exact major and hobbies—missing brilliant collaborators just outside your immediate circle.

**Together**, they create **intelligent serendipity**: people you'll genuinely work with, not just people who look good on paper.

## What You Can Do

### Improve Your Matches

1. **Be specific in your bio** — "Building climate tech" beats "Working on projects." The AI learns more.
2. **Fill in all sections** — The four dimensions use all available data. More detail = better matches.
3. **Be honest about what you want** — Describe the people you actually want to meet, not who you think you should meet.

### Understand Your Scores

- **85%+** — High potential for deep collaboration
- **70–84%** — Good connection, mutual value
- **Below 70%** — Worth exploring if a specific dimension is very high (e.g., high Resonance even if Intent is lower)

## A Quick Example

**You:** "Building an AI tool for clinical research. Interested in biology + tech collaboration."

**The AI understands:**
- You're entrepreneurial, technical, interested in healthcare impact
- You want collaborators, not competitors
- You value precision and scientific thinking

**A match, Sarah:**
- Geography: Different college (−2 points)
- Subject: Biology (your field) = +12 points
- Goals: "Research collaborators in computational biology" = +15 points (complements yours)
- Resonance: Loves hiking and philosophy, you love hiking and reading (shared interest) = +8 points
- Network: Both in the Biology Society (shared community) = +12 points

**Result:** Sarah isn't your twin, but she's a *meaningful* match. You'd likely collaborate well.

## Under the Hood (Optional Technical Context)

**For the curious:** Supercharged uses vector embeddings—a technique that converts your profile text into mathematical representations that capture meaning. Two profiles with similar embeddings (even if they use different words) will match as semantically similar. This is combined with traditional scoring on the four dimensions to produce a final compatibility score.

But you don't need to know that to benefit from it. Just fill out your profile honestly and thoughtfully.
